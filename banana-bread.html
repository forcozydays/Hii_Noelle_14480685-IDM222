Banana Bread

Ingredients

  • 3 very ripe bananas, mashed
  • 1/3 cup (75 g) melted butter
  • 1/2 cup (100 g) granulated sugar
  • 1/4 cup (50 g) brown sugar
  • 1 large egg
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 1 teaspoon baking soda
  • Pinch of salt
  • 1 1/2 cups (190 g) all-purpose flour

How To

  • Preheat oven to 350°F (175°C). Grease or line a 9x5-inch loaf pan.
  • In a large bowl, mash the bananas with a fork until mostly smooth.
  • Stir in the melted butter.
  • Mix in the sugars, egg, and vanilla until combined.
  • Sprinkle in the baking soda and salt, then mix.
  • Add the flour and stir just until incorporated.
  • Pour batter into the prepared loaf pan.
  • Bake for 50 to 60 minutes, or until a toothpick inserted in the center comes out clean.
  • Let cool in the pan for 10 to 15 minutes, then transfer to a wire rack to cool completely.
