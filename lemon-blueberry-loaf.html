Lemon Blueberry Yogurt Loaf

Jump to Recipe

This loaf is bright, tender, and bursting with juicy blueberries. The yogurt keeps it moist, and the lemon zest makes every bite fresh and happy. It’s a perfect morning treat, afternoon snack, or something sweet to share with friends.

Why yogurt makes it so moist

Full-fat yogurt adds richness and tenderness without making the loaf heavy. It also gives a subtle tang that enhances the lemon flavor.

Fresh vs. frozen blueberries

Both work! If using frozen, do not thaw them first — toss them lightly in flour and fold in gently to prevent streaking.

How to boost lemon flavor

Rubbing lemon zest into the sugar before mixing releases fragrant oils and intensifies the citrus aroma throughout the loaf.

The finishing touch

A simple lemon glaze made with powdered sugar and fresh lemon juice adds sweetness and a beautiful glossy finish.

Ingredients

  • All-Purpose Flour (190g)
  • Baking Powder (1.5 teaspoons)
  • Salt (0.5 teaspoon).
  • Granulated Sugar (200g).
  • Lemon Zest (from 1 large lemon).
  • Eggs (2 large, room temperature).
  • Plain Greek Yogurt (120g, full-fat).
  • Vegetable Oil (120ml): Keeps crumb soft.
  • Vanilla Extract (1 teaspoon).
  • Fresh Blueberries (150g).

How To

  • Preheat oven to 350F and line a loaf pan.
  • Rub lemon zest into sugar until fragrant.
  • Whisk in eggs, yogurt, oil, and vanilla.
  • Stir in flour, baking powder, and salt.
  • Fold in blueberries gently.
  • Bake 50 to 60 minutes until a toothpick comes out clean.
  • Cool completely before glazing.
